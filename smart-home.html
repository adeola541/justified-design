NSB FOOD MOBILE APP

Nigeria Spicy Burger (NSB) is a dynamic and user-friendly mobile app designed to redefine how customers order and enjoy Nigerian-inspired spicy burgers. It allows for quick ordering, robust cart management, and advanced flavor customizations.

OVERVIEW

The goal of the NSB Food App was to digitize a local, culturally-rich fast food experience while solving complex logistical problems typical in the region (like delivery tracking and localized payment gateways). The app features an intuitive UI prioritizing large food photography and simple tap-to-add mechanisms.

DESIGN PROCESS

1. Discovery & Research: Conducted market analysis on local fast-food chains and identified gaps in user experience, particularly around checkout speed and menu clarity.

2. Wireframing: Developed low-fidelity sketches outlining the core user journey: Home -> Category -> Product Details -> Cart -> Checkout.

3. High-Fidelity & Usability: Applied the brand's vibrant colors (Deep Oranges and Reds) mimicking spices, creating a mouth-watering aesthetic that triggers appetite. Tested with 15 target demographic users.
